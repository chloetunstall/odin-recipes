Vegetarian Chili

Description

An easy and hearty dinner! Packed not only with vegetables, but with flavor as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small or medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 jalapeño peppers, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 2 (4 oz) cans of green chile peppers, chopped
  • 24 oz vegetarian burger crumbles
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of tomato sauce
  • chili powder, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (15 oz) can of kidney beans
  • 1 (15 oz) can of pinto beans
  • 1 (15 oz) can of black beans
  • 1 (15 oz) can of great northern beans
  • 1 (15 oz) can of whole kernel corn

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the onion and season with the oregano, basil, salt, and cumin.
  3. Cook and stir until onion is tender.
  4. Stir in the bell peppers, jalapeños, and garlic.
  5. Add green chile peppers and cook until heated through.
  6. Stir in vegetarian burger crumples, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in the peeled tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, pinto beans, black beans, great northern beans, chili powder, and black pepper.
  8. Bring the pot to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 45 minutes.
  9. Stir in the corn and continue to cook for about 5 minutes.
  10. Serve and enjoy your home-cooked meal!
