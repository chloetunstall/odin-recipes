Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

A super simple dessert that you and your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Mix peanut butter, white sugar, and egg in a medium bowl until the mixture is smooth.
  4. Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls, placing them 1 inch apart on the parchment-paper-lined baking sheet.
  5. Flatten each of the balls with a fork, leaving the iconic criss-cross pattern.
  6. Bake for 6-8 minutes, until cookies are just browning on the bottom.
  7. Move to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Serve and enjoy your home-made dessert!
